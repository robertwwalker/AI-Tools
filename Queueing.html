Serverless M/M/c Simulation with Matplotlib

1. The Staffing Challenge: $\lambda$, $\mu$, and $c$

Healthcare systems are plagued by variability. We use the **M/M/c** model to understand how arrival bursts interact with service times. The key to everything is System Utilization ($\rho$), the ratio of work arriving versus work that can be done:

$$\rho = \frac{\lambda}{c \cdot \mu}$$

If $\rho \ge 1$, the system fails. As a manager, your job is to visualize the optimal balance. This simulation will help you find the 'elbow' where adding one more doctor ($c$) dramatically slashes wait times.
